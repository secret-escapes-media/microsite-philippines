Head over to Cebu, the heart of the Visayas island group.

Buzzing Cebu City is a riotous stomping ground of delectable food, electric bars and high-rise modernity, whereas the rest of the island offers peaceful retreats of typical Filipino splendour. Known for great diving spots and enigmatic natural features, making Cebu a great island destination.
